Welcome to the Brain Atlas for X-ALD

This educational website is designed to provide an interactive learning experience using the anatomical atlas tailored for MRI severity scoring in X-linked adrenoleukodystrophy (X-ALD).

Developed by researchers at the Bellvitge University Hospital, Institut de Diagnostic per la Imatge, and IDIBELL "Neurometabolic diseases" and "Diagnostic Imaging and Nuclear Medicine" Research groups.

Explore Brain Regions

  • FRONTAL: Deep, Middle, and Superficial layers of the frontal lobe.
  • PARIETO-OCCIPITAL: Deep, Middle, and Superficial layers of the parieto-occipital regions lobe.
  • ANTERIOR TEMPORAL: Deep, Middle, and Superficial layers of the anterior temporal lobe.
  • CORPUS CALLOSUM: Body, Genu, and Splenium.
  • THALAMUS: Anterior thalamus.
  • CORTICOSPINAL: Corticospinal tract separated by brainstem and internal capsule regions.
  • ACOUSTIC: Brachium, Lateral Lemniscus, Medial Geniculate ganglion, Pons.
  • OPTIC: Lateral Geniculate, Meyers Loop, Radiations.
  • MISC: Basal Ganglia, Cerebellum.
